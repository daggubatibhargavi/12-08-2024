Making Tea

  1. Boil Water in a pot
  1. Add tea leaves and sugar
  1. Let it simmer for a few minutes

  2. Strain tea into cup and add milk if desired

Making Paratha and Aloo curry

  1. Knead the dought for paratha and let it rest
  1. Roll out the dough and cook on a hot griddle with oil or ghee
  1. For aloo curry,saute onions,tomatoes,and spices in a pan

  2. Add boiled potatoes and cook untill the curry thickens

Making Biriyani

  1. Marinate the meat and vegetables with spices and yogurt
  1. Cook basmati rice untill its 70% done.
  1. Layer the marinated meat vegetables and rice in a pot.

  2. Cook on low heat(dum)untill fully cooked.
